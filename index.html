TCNJ Magic Circle Game Design


Magic Circle was created before the IMM department had an official Games class. Many students came together wishing to learn more about game development, and so they started this club

The Magic Circle describes the psycological and physiological state a player is in while a game is in progress; a 'seperate reality'.

"I'm don't plan to be a designer; is this club still for me?"

Of course! Our member base is not limited to any one career goal. We need the talents of people from all disciplines to collaborate on our projects.

"I'm not interested in video games. Is that all you work on?"

No! We are interested in all sorts of games, from board games and card games to alternate-reality games and video games.
