Magic Circle @ TCNJ game design / game news / gaming

Are you interested in game design?
Do you aspire to work in the games industry?
The Magic Circle is a club for TCNJ's game designers to meet, share ideas, learn new things, and work on projects with their peers.
Join us Thursday nights at 9pm in AIMM 202.

"I'm not planning to be a game designer, is this club still for me?"

Of course! Our member base is not limited to any one career goal. We need the talents of people from all disciplines to collaborate on our projects.

"I'm not really interested in video games. Is that all you work on?"

No! We are interested in all sorts of games, from board games and card games to alternate-reality games and video games.
