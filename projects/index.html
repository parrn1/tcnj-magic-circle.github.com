GRACE

GRACE is a big game, simulating the invasion of a planet by an intergalactic corporation.

You can play as the corporation, the planet, the native population, or as chance. Each of the four players has a very unique role to play. Any number of the players can win, from all to none.

We hope to release it in a variety of formats, including tabletop and computer versions.

Kairos

Kairos is an innovative puzzle game in which the player is gifted the power to control the forwards and backwards passage of time unique to objects in his or her world. These objects exist in two categories: Manmade and Nature. While advancing time on the manmade structures will cause them to deteriorate and eventually decay, nature will in contrast grow and flourish. Your hands over time will be your power to solve puzzles.

Kairos began as a game developed in 48 hours at the Philly Game Jam 2012 and has been in production since.

Aurora

Aurora is a space-shooter, similar to Galaga or Galactix. The similarities end at 'space-shooter' though, as Aurora is a collaborative assault in which 4 players all control different parts of one ship (1 driver, 3 gunners)! It takes true teamwork (and a few computer screens) to be able to master this fast-paced shooter.
